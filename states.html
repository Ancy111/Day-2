Rajasthan

Rajasthan boasts stunning tourist spots like the Amber Fort and City Palace in Jaipur, the lake city of Udaipur, the golden dunes of Jaisalmer, and the blue city of Jodhpur. Wildlife enthusiasts can visit Ranthambore National Park, while Pushkar is a spiritual hub with its holy lake and Brahma Temple.

Kerala

Kerala offers tranquil backwaters in Alleppey, scenic hill stations like Munnar, and wildlife sanctuaries in Thekkady. Kovalam and Varkala beaches attract beachgoers, while Wayanad's greenery and the historic port city of Kochi add to its charm.

Goa

Goa is famous for its beaches like Baga, Calangute, and Palolem, along with historical sites like Basilica of Bom Jesus and Fort Aguada. Water sports, vibrant markets, and Dudhsagar Waterfalls make it a must-visit destination.

Himachal Pradesh

Himachal Pradesh features Shimla’s colonial charm, Manali’s snow-capped peaks, and Dharamshala’s Tibetan culture. Trekking routes in Kasol, the serene Spiti Valley, and adventure activities in Solang Valley are major attractions.

Tamil Nadu

Tamil Nadu’s attractions include the temples of Mahabalipuram and Madurai, the hill station of Ooty, and the beaches of Rameswaram and Kanyakumari. The French-influenced town of Pondicherry and Chennai’s Marina Beach are also popular.

Uttarakhand

Uttarakhand offers spiritual spots like Kedarnath, Badrinath, and Haridwar. Rishikesh attracts adventure seekers with rafting and yoga, while Nainital, Mussoorie, and Auli offer serene hill station experiences.

Uttar Pradesh

Uttar Pradesh is home to the Taj Mahal in Agra, Varanasi’s sacred ghats, and the historic Fatehpur Sikri. Mathura and Vrindavan are spiritual hubs, while Lucknow showcases Nawabi culture and cuisine.
